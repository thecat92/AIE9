
# Stress Management Guide

## Overview
Stress management helps improve mental and physical well-being.

## Evidence-Based Techniques
1. Mindfulness meditation
2. Regular physical activity
3. Deep breathing exercises
4. Cognitive behavioral strategies
5. Healthy sleep routines

## 7-Day Starter Plan
Day 1-2: Breathing + short walks  
Day 3-4: Add mindfulness  
Day 5-7: Combine all techniques
